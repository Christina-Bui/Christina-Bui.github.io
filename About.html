Hi there!

It's me!

I'm currently a second-year Digital Media Master's student at Georgia Tech. My work focuses on the development of new media and user experience design. I love what I do because design is an outlet for the progression of the things that are most important to me: human relationships, self-development, and the act of making.

When I'm not designing, I love drinking tea, playing video games, drawing, and dreaming of all of the cats I'll befriend when I get my own space.

Research
