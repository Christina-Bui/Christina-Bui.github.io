    I'm kind of all over the place. I love to explore and try new things. As a student, this means taking an interest in a variety of subjects ranging from ancient Greek to planet formation to music theory. As a person, this means being open to new experiences, especially ones that wouldn't normally be considered positive and finding the positivity in them. I'm the only person I know who would laugh at getting a flat tire.

    My passion for design stems from this positivity. For a long time, the idea of pursuing an artistic career path was very unrealistic, but I was adamant in finding a way to make that fantasy reality. Today, I've made it so that I can be a designer without anything in my way.

    When I'm not watching tutorials, you can find me having a hot cup of Earl Grey in my room.
